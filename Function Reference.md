| Concept           | Definition                         | Formula |
|------------------|---------------------------------|---------|
| **Loss Function** | Error for **one** data point    | $\mathcal{L}(y, \hat{y})$ |
| **Cost Function** | Average (or sum) of all losses | $J(\theta) = \frac{1}{N} \sum \mathcal{L}(y_i, \hat{y}_i)$ |
